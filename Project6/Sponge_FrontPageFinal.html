How to use a sponge

How to use a sponge for cleaning objects

  • Step 1: Grab the sponge.
  • Step 2: Wet the sponge.
  • Step 3: Squeeze out excess water.
  • Step 4: Use different sides for cleaning.

How to use a sponge for cleaning dishes

  • Step 1: Wet the sponge.
  • Step 2: Apply dish soap.
  • Step 3: Scrub dishes with the soft side.
  • Step 4: Use the scrubby side for tougher stains.
  • Step 5: Rinse dishes, squeeze out excess water from the sponge, and let dishes air dry.
