Interesting Sea Sponge Species

A sea sponge is a multicellular, aquatic organism belonging to the phylum Porifera, characterized by a porous body structure. These simple aquatic animals lack true tissues and organs but play a crucial role in marine ecosystems by filtering water and providing habitats for various organisms. Harvested for various purposes, natural sea sponges are often used commercially for bathing, cleaning, and painting.
